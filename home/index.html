Welcome to RexiMemo!



What is RexiMemo?

RexiMemo is a reboot of the (now closed) service Flipnote Hatena for Flipnote Studio on the Nintendo DSi!


How do I connect to RexiMemo?

You can connect to RexiMemo by follwing the guide located in the How to Connect area of the site.


What can I use to access RexiMemo?

RexiMemo can be accessed via any console capable of running Nintendo DSiWare (Nintendo DSi, Nintendo DSi XL, Nintendo 3DS, New Nintendo 3DS etc.) and via the melonDS emulator using a Nintendo DSi NAND & firmware dump.
